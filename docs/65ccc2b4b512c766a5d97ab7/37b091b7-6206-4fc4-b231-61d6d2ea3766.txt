ADD MEMBERS TO PROJECTS AND ORGANIZATIONS



If you are an owner, you can add members to your organizations and projects. If
you are a , you can add members to your project.


ADD MEMBERS TO A PROJECT

 1. Open the .
 2. Select your project.
 3. Click Members.
 4. Click + Invite Member.
 5. Choose a for the member.
 6. Enter the member's email address.
 7. Click Invite Member.


ADD MEMBERS TO AN ORGANIZATION

 1. Open the .
 2. Go to Settings.
 3. On the Members tab, click + Invite Member.
 4. Choose an for the member.
 5. For an organization owner, enter the owner's email address. For an
    organization user, choose a ) and enter the user's email address.
 6. Click Invite Member.

When you invite another user to join your organization or project, Pinecone
sends them an email containing a link that enables them to gain access to the
organization or project. If they already have a Pinecone account, they still
receive an email, but they can also immediately view the project.
