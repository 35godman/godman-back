PODS

An index is made up of pods, which are units of cloud resources (vCPU, RAM,
disk) that provide storage and compute for each index. Choose the that works
best for your use case.


INDEXES

Indexes store your vector embeddings and metadata. Each index uses at least one
pod, but you can add more to increase storage capacity.


SCALING

As you grow, you can scale storage capacity by increasing your pod sizes. You
can also increase or decrease throughput when needed, by adding to an index.
