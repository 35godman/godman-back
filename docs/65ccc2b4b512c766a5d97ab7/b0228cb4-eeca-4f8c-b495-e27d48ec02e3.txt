INTRODUCTION

Vector search has been used by tech giants like Google and Amazon for decades.
It has been claimed to be a significant driver in clicks, views, and sales
across several platforms. Yet, it was only with Faiss that this technology
became more accessible.

In the past few years, vector search exploded in popularity. It has driven
ecommerce sales, powered music and podcast search, and even recommended your
next favorite shows on streaming platforms. Vector search is everywhere and in
the following chapters you will discover why it has found such great success and
how to apply it yourself using the Facebook AI Similarity Search (Faiss)
library.
