DATADOG



This page shows you how to use Datadog to monitor your Pinecone vector database.


> ℹ️
> 
> NOTE
> 
> This feature is available only for pod-based indexes on Enterprise or
> Enterprise Dedicated plans. Serverless indexes do not currently support
> Datadog monitoring.


SET UP YOUR PINECONE INTEGRATION ON DATADOG

Follow these steps to set up your Pinecone integration:

 1. Log in to .
 2. Go to > .
 3. In the search bar, enter 'Pinecone'.
 4. Click Pinecone Integration.
 5. Click Install.
 6. Under Pinecone Accounts, click Add new.
 7. Enter the , , and for your project.
 8. In the left navigation menu, click Dashboards.
 9. Under All Dashboards, click Pinecone Overview. This dashboard shows basic
    monitoring data for your Pinecone vector database.
