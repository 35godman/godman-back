CHANGING YOUR BILLING PLAN



This document describes how to change the billing plan for your Pinecone
organization through the Pinecone console..


> ℹ️
> 
> NOTE
> 
> Accounts created by must change billing plans through the Pinecone console
> using this workflow.

To change your billing plan, you must be the for your organization.

To change your billing plan through the Pinecone console, follow these steps:

 1. Log in to the .
 2. In the left menu, click Organizations.
 3. Click the Billing tab.
 4. Under the plan you want, click Upgrade.
