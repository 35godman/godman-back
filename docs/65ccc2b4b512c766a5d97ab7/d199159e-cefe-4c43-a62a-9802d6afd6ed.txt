> ℹ️
> 
> INFO
> 
> Pod limits do not apply to [serverless indexes](indexes#serverless-indexes.
> Pinecone auto-scales serverless indexes based on usage, and you pay only for
> what you use.

If you are a , follow these steps to set or change the maximum total number of
allowed across all pod-based indexes in your project:

 1. Open the .
 2. Go to Settings.
 3. On the Projects tab, click the Configure Project icon next to the project
    you want to update.
 4. Enter a Pod Limit. The default pod limit is 5.
 5. Click Save Changes.
