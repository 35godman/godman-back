ADD USERS TO PROJECTS AND ORGANIZATIONS



OVERVIEW

If you are a or owner, follow these steps to add users to organizations and
projects.


ADD USERS TO PROJECTS AND ORGANIZATIONS

 1. Access the .

 2. Click Settings in the left menu.

 3. In the Settings view, click the USERS tab.

 4. Click +INVITE USER.

 5. (Organization owner only) Select an .

 6. Select one or more projects.

 7. Select a .

 8. Enter the user's email address.

 9. Click +INVITE USER.

When you invite another user to join your organization or project, Pinecone
sends them an email containing a link that enables them to gain access to the
organization or project. If they already have a Pinecone account, they still
receive an email, but they can also immediately view the project.
