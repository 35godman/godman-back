MONITORING YOUR USAGE



This document describes how to monitor the usage and costs for your Pinecone
organization through the Pinecone console.

To view your Pinecone usage, you must be the for your organization. This feature
is only available to organizations on the Standard or Enterprise plans.

To view your usage through the Pinecone console, follow these steps:

 1. Log in to the .
 2. In the left menu, click Organizations.
 3. Click the USAGE tab.

All dates are given in UTC to match billing invoices.
