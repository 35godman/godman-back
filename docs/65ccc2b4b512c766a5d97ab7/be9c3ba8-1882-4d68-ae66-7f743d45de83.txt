SETTING UP BILLING THROUGH AWS MARKETPLACE



This document describes how to configure pay-as-you-go billing for your Pinecone
organization through Amazon Web Services (AWS) Marketplace. To commit to annual
spending, .


> ℹ️
> 
> NOTE
> 
> This workflow creates a new Pinecone . If you already have an organization,
> signing up through AWS Marketplace creates an additional organization.

To configure Pinecone billing through the AWS Marketplace, follow these steps:

 1. Log in to the . Your project must be enabled for purchase by your billing
    administrator.
 2. Search for .
 3. Click Subscribe.
 4. Read and agree to the terms and conditions.
 5. Click Subscribe.
 6. Click the Set up your account button.
 7. Sign in to Pinecone.io.

When you sign in, Pinecone creates a new organization linked to your AWS
billing.

If you already have a Pinecone organization, you can select the new "AWS Linked"
organization in the organization drop-down menu in the console.
